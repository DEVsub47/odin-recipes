Lasagna


Description

Casseroles are a traditional food containing various ingredients ranging from vegetables to meats to pasta which are all made into a single pie like food.


Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Pasta
  • Beef
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients in a a bowl and mix thoroughly
  2. Pour the mixture into baking tray
  3. Preheat the oven to 400c and wait for 40 minutes
